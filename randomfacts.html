In a cavern in the deep, dark, unlocated forest, Felix found a golden chest, but there was a keyhole. He had to find the key quick, or else a cage full of venomous cobras would open up, killing Felix. There was a skeleton near the wall of the cave. There was 1:55 left on the clock. The skeleton held the key, and it was inside it's ribcage. So Felix reached into the ribcage, took the key, and inserted it into the chest. He twisted it, and it opened. He pulled out a scroll, and guess what it said. Random Facts written at the top. By the time Felix was finished reading the scroll, the cobras dropped down and killed him.

241 years later...

Camp Leader Mr. Pam found the scroll located in the cave, crusty with venom. He then he posted it on Twitter. This how I found these amazing random facts and other stuff!
